We are more than just a group - we're a family united by faith, strengthened by friendship, and committed to being each other's rock of refuge in every season of life.

Our Mission

To create a welcoming community where individuals can grow in their faith, build meaningful relationships, and support one another through life's journey while serving God and our community with love and dedication.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and love in our community, where every member experiences the transformative power of God's love and becomes equipped to be a rock of refuge for others in their spiritual journey.
